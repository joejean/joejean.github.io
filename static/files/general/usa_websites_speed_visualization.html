Speed and Usability Performance of Top US Websites

This visualization presents an interactive graph showing the speed and usability scores – on both desktop and mobile – of the most popular US websites according to Alexa. The performance data is dynamically computed with Google PageSpeed Insights.

Please be patient. The page takes a while to load the data and render the graph.

The visualization is interactive! Please click on the table header to sort by speed score, name or usability score.

This is a work in progress, I plan to add more features to it. Your suggestions are welcomed. Thank you!
